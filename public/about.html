The Velo Mission

We believe that sharing something with the person next to you shouldn't involve a trip around the world.

Why we exist

Cloud services are great for storage, but terrible for instant transfer. They impse limits, require sign-ups, and track your data. Velo was built to be the anti-cloud. We use WebRTC to create a direct data tunnel between peers.

Values

  • Speed above all else.
  • Privacy by default.
  • Zero friction.

Tech Stack

  • Node.js & WebSocket (Signaling)
  • WebRTC (Data Channels)
  • Vanilla JS (Performance)
