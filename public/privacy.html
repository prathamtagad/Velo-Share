Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

At PyShare, we believe in privacy by design. This policy outlines how we handle your dataâ€”or rather, how we don't.

1. The Short Version

We do not store your files. We do not track your transfers. We do not sell your data. PyShare is a peer-to-peer service, meaning files go directly from you to the recipient.

2. Data We Collect

We collect only the bare minimum technical data required to establish connections:

  • Temporary Signaling Data: To connect two peers, we must temporarily relay "signaling" messages (SDP offers/answers, ICE candidates) via our server. These messages contain networking information (like IP addresses) required for WebRTC. They are not stored and are discarded immediately after relay.
  • Room Metadata: We keep track of active room codes and the number of connected peers in that room in-memory. This data is wiped when the room is destroyed.

3. File Storage

We do not store any files on our servers.

Files are streamed directly from the sender's browser to the receiver's browser using WebRTC Data Channels. Detailed file content never touches our infrastructure.

4. Analytics

We do not use third-party analytics trackers (like Google Analytics) or cookies on this site.

5. Contact

If you have any questions about this policy, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.
